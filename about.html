Manage Your Life

Manage Your Life was born from a personal problem: the difficulty of organizing my life using Excel spreadsheets, manual calculations, and fragmented controls. From this real need, I decided to create a digital solution capable of centralizing information, automating calculations, and providing a clear and objective view of personal organization and productivity.

Despite my focus on back-end, I have also acquired front-end knowledge — such as HTML and CSS, as well as basic JavaScript — to have autonomy in creating my own pages and complete applications.
I have experience solving real problems in professional environments, especially through automation, always aiming to optimize processes, reduce manual work, and increase efficiency.
